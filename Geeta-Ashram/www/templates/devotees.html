Revered Swami Guru Maa Geeteshwari Ji


Swami Guru Maa Geeteshwari ji as born in may 1949 to a traditional and religious Hindu family. Her family hails from Pune a city in the State of Maharashtra where She went on to complete Her primary Education. On Graduating from Saint Meera College in Pune, she took to working as a teacher in Saint Meera School for the next five years.

She moved base after Her Marriage in 1971 to Monrovia, the Capital of Liberia situated in West Africa. Her husband was self employed and running his business during this time.

In the year 1975, by the grace of God, She encountered the opportunity to attend a discourse being given by Swami Hari Har ji Maharaj in Liberia. This was the beginning of Her dedication and binding for Lord Krishna and a beginning to her journey in spiritualism. In 1977, on the eve of Holy Purnima and Guru Maharaj’s Birthday she keenly took initiation under the guidance of Guru ji and became his formal disciple. Subsequently She became the Secretary of Geeta Ashram in Liberia for ten consecutive years and showed devotion to Guru ji, Bhagvad Geeta and Lord Krishna.

In wake of the civil war that started in Liberia in the year 1990, alike other Indian immigrants, Guru Maa returned to India with her husband and two children. After settling in India, She witnessed the most turbulent period in her life on the sudden demise of Her husband. During this phase of grief and sorrow, She showed immense courage and composure towards her duty to her family. She turned to Bhagvad Geeta in Her solitude and once again came in contact with Shri Guru Maharaj through engaging in religious activities.

In March 2000, Guru Maharaj took salvation and attained Moksha. Since then Guru Maa pledged Her Life to Bhagvad Geeta by denouncing all worldly pleasures and has enhanced her knowledge on Vedas, Shastras, Upanishads and Astrology. She actively promotes the knowledge of Bhagvad Geeta across the globe.

She has been conferred the title of Guru Maa, for the extreme devotion She showed towards Her unconditional motherly Love for Guru Maharaj in the last twelve years of His Life. Under the direction and guidance of Guru Maharaj She adapted the Bhagvad Geeta by compassion and memorised every verse. She gained profound knowledge of the Geeta, its content and the message being conveyed through this Holy Book. Being an exponent of the Bhagvad Geeta, Guru Maharaj had also given Her the title of “Geeta Ratna”, “Geeta Bhaskar” and “Geeteshwari”.

In recent years, Guru Maa has been vigorously traveling and spreading the message of Geeta via the 600 Ashrams placed in different countries. She has given up all worldly desires and material possessions to serve Her Guru and Lord Krishna by mind and Spirit. Her priority has been the Geeta Family and spreading the Message of Gopal. Her proficiency has been highlighted in Her writings, “Amritvani” and “Gurudev’s exemplary experiences”.

Her skill of using simple and understandable language in a result of Her learning under late Guru ji. Guru Maa has simplified all the eighteen Chapters of Geeta for mass interest as She holds the notion that simplicity produces easy assimilation of content and increases interest in learning. The objective of Bhagvad Geeta has been explicitly explained so that the message reaches one and all. Certain excerpts have been taken from Guru Maa’s writings to provide an example of simple language. “Do all your tasks keeping in mind that you are serving Gopal” “Keep doing your work and chanting the name of Gopal” “Reading, chanting and memorising Bhagvad Geeta unites human beings with God” “Whoever thinks of me, I free him of all worries” .



Acharya Shri Jai Prakash Mishra Ji



Geeta Ashram Thailand is fortunate to have a revered saint whose name is ‘Acharya Shri Jai Prakash Mishra Ji’ (or "Pujari ji" as he is dearly referred to by his followers). Coming from a very humble background, he has achieved marvelous knowlegde, understanding and insight of the underlying meanings of each and every verse in the ‘Song of God,' the Shrimad Bhagwat Geeta. Recognized and inducted by His Holiness Swami Hari Har Ji Maharaj, Acharya Shri Jai Prakash Ji has elevated himself to divinity and the state of blissfulness. He dwells in this world with ease and harmonizes his life in line with the teachings of the Shrimad Bhagwat Geeta. He commands himself to the daily practices of Purity and Sheels (Devine laws), staying true to his sainthood. All regular activities related to all devotees are taken care by him with the support of the Geeta Ashram Managing Committee, as supervised by His Holiness Swami Hari Har Ji Maharaj.
